- Datasets
	- The Diabetes dataset 
		- Data originally collected by the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases.
		- Data downloadable from: https://github.com/MicrosoftDocs/ml-basics/blob/master/data/diabetes.csv
	- Breast Cancer Wisconsin dataset
		- Data originally collected by the University of Wisconsin Hospitals, Madison
		- Data downloadable from: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/breast+cancer+wisconsin+(diagnostic)
